I was able to get an inside look into game protgramming, and the tech culture in general. I quickly fell in love with the idea of programming, and the creativity that came with it. I soon began teaching myself how to code, and making my own games with the newly found knowledge. In my junior year I led my team in goals, and was named offensive MVP. Unfortunately, my senior year was cut short due to the covid pandemic. I am also part of the lacrosse team at CSULB.
